I am an Associate Professor of Economics in the [Martha and Spencer Love School of Business](https://www.elon.edu/u/academics/business/) at [Elon University](https://www.elon.edu). I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Kentucky in 2012.

My research spans two areas. In **international economics**, I study how countries transition through stages of development, focusing on the relationship between economic growth takeoffs and the structure of international trade. In **economics education**, I investigate how active learning strategies and technology shape student outcomes.
